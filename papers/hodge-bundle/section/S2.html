Section 2. The proof

Suppose that $\mathscr{V}\subset\Omega_{g}$ is a sub-bundle of rank $r>0$, and let $S$ be the set of integers $m$ such that there exists a genus $g$ curve admitting an involution with exactly $2m$ fixed points.

Consider the function $f:S\to\mathbb{Z}$ which maps $m$ to the trace of the involution $\tau^{*}$ on the fiber $V_C$ of $\mathscr{V}$ for any curve $C$ admitting an involution $\tau$ with $2m$ fixed points. (This is well-defined by Lemma 2.1.)
