Section 1. Introduction

The Hodge bundle $\Omega_{g}$ is the rank $g$ vector bundle over the moduli stack of complex genus $g$ curves $\mathscr{M}_{g}$ which assigns to a curve $C\in\mathscr{M}_{g}$ its vector space $H^{0}(C,\omega_{C})$ of holomorphic differentials. 1 In this note, we prove that $\Omega_{g}$ is simple, meaning:

Theorem 1.1. (E0AE)
The Hodge bundle $\Omega_{g}$ does not contain any non-trivial sub-bundles.

We will contextualize Theorem 1.1 after some important acknowledgments.
