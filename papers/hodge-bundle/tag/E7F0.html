Lemma 2.5. (E7F0)

Let $G$ be a finite group acting on a finite-dimensional vector space $V$, and let $\chi$ denote a character of $G$. Let $V^{\chi}$ denote the sub-representation of $V$ on which $G$ acts via scaling by $\chi$. Then $$ \dim V^{\chi}=\frac{1}{|G|}\sum_{g}\chi(g)^{-1}\operatorname{tr}(g). $$
