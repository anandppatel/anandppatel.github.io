Definition 2.3. (2967)

Suppose $\iota: \P^{1} \to \P^{n}$ is a linear map such that $\iota^{*}F \in \k[s,t]$ is non-zero and divisible by $s^{k}$. A first order deformation $\iota_{\epsilon}$ of $\iota$ preserves order $k$ contact at $[0:1]$ if $\iota_{\epsilon}^{*}F$ is divisible by $s^{k}$ in the ring $\k[\epsilon,s,t]/(\epsilon^{2})$.
