Proof of Claim 3.17 [BD5C]. (2D76)

We use the same idea as in the proof of Lemma 3.15 [8893]. Complete $L^\bullet_{j-1}$ and $G^\bullet=(0 \subset W_{j-1}|_{0} \subset V_{j-1}|_0)$ to generic complete flags $\widehat{L}^\bullet_{j-1}$ and $\widehat{G}^\bullet$. Since $G^\bullet$ is transverse to $L^\bullet_{j-1}$ by assumption, so is $\widehat{L}^\bullet_{j-1}$ and $\widehat{G}^\bullet$. Choose $v_i$ such that $\langle v_i \rangle = \widehat{L}^i_{j-1} \cap \widehat{G}^{n-i-1}$.
