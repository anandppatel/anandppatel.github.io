Remark 1.2. (D8A2)

Before considering the syzygy bundles $N_{i}$, one might ask about the generic behavior of the Tschirnhausen bundle $E$. A theorem of Coppens [Cop99] and Ballico [Bal89] shows that $E$ is balanced for a generic cover.
