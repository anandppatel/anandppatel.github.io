Lemma 2.5. (1606)

Let $E \subset \P^{d-1}$ be a degree $d$ smooth elliptic normal curve, let $H$ be a hyperplane in $\P^{d-2}$, and let $Z := H \cap E$.

  1. If $$0 \to F_{1} \to ... \to F_{k}\to {\sh I}_{E \subset \P^{d-1}} \to 0$$ is the minimal free resolution of $E \subset \P^{d-1}$, then $$0 \to F_{1}|_{H} \to ... \to F_{k}|_{H} \to {\sh I}_{Z \subset H} \to 0$$ is the minimal free resolution of $Z \subset H$.
  2. The syzygy bundles $F_{i}$ appearing in the minimal free resolution (1) are of the form $\cO_{\P^{d-1}}(-m_{i})^{\oplus r_{i}}$ for some integers $m_{i}$ and $r_{i}$, i.e. all summands of $F_{i}$ have the same degree.
