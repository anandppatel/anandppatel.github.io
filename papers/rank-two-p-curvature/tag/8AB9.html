Proof. (8AB9)

The $f$-local system $L$ gives, by Proposition 3.12 [8EAA], a monodromy representation $$\begin{aligned}\rho_{L}: \underline{\pi_{1}(C/B)} \to \Aut(p^{*}{L})\end{aligned}$$ which factors through $\underline{\pi_{1}(X/B)}$ over the dense open subset $U \subset B$ by hypothesis. By continuity, $\rho_{L}$ must factor through $\underline{\pi_{1}(X/B)}$ on all of $B$, and therefore defines (by Proposition 3.13 [496D]) the required $f$-local system $M$.
