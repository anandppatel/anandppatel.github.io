Remark 3.2. (0DB1)

We can extend the vector bundle $V$ (which lives on $C|_{B \setminus E}$) trivially to all of $C$ using the cyclic basis ${\mathbf{e}}$. Then, in order to prove Theorem 1.6 [A99E], it suffices to show that the connection matrix of $\nabla$ with respect to $\mathbf{e}$ has no poles in $q$.
