Theorem 1.5. (6BC3)

Suppose $C$ is a genus $0$ curve with $n$ generic punctures, and let $(V,\nabla)$ be a rank 2 vector bundle with connection on $C$ with $p$-curvatures vanishing for almost all prime numbers $p$. If $P \subset C(\C)^{an}$ is any pair of pants, then $(V,\nabla)^{an}$ restricted to $P$ has finite monodromy.
