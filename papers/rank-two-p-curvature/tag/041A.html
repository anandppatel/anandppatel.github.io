Corollary 1.7. (041A)

Maintain the notation above and let $b \in B$ denote a codimension-one point of $B \setminus B^{o}$. If the $p$-curvatures of $(V,\nabla)$ vanish for almost all primes $p$, then there exists an étale neighbourhood $U$ of $b$ such that the pair $(V,\nabla)$ extends to an analytic vector bundle on $X \times_B U$ with connection relative to $U$.
