Proof. (9488)

If $(V, \nabla)$ is a vector bundle with connection on $X$ whose $p$-curvatures vanish for almost all $p$, then the pullback $(p^{*}V, p^{*}\nabla)$ induces a vector bundle with connection relative to $B$ with the same $p$-curvature vanishing condition. Since (by condition $(2)$) the map to moduli $B \to [\mathcal{M}_{g,n}/ S_{n}]$ is dominant, a general fiber $C_b$ is a generic curve, and therefore Theorem curve applies. Hence, $(p^{*}V,p^{*}\nabla)|_{C_{b}}$ has finite monodromy, which (by condition $(1)$) implies that $(V,\nabla)$ also has finite monodromy.
