Definition 3.4. (79BA)

Let $F(S)$ denote the fraction field of $S$, and let $D \in \Der_R(F(S))$. Let $\nu$ denote a discrete valuation on $F(S)$. We say that the derivation $D'$ is $\nu$-integral if $\nu(D(\alpha)) \geq \nu(\alpha)$ for all $\alpha \in F(S)$.
