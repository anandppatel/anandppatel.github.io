Theorem 2.8. (3A23)

{top0} Let $\Sigma$ be a topological surface of genus zero with $n\geq0$ punctures. If a semisimple representation $\rho:\pi_1\Sigma\to\GL_2(\C)$ has finite restriction to every pair of pants on $\Sigma$, then the image of $\rho$ is finite.
