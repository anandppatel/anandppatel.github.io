Proposition 3.13. (496D)

Let $W$ be a vector bundle on $B$, and $\rho: \underline{\pi_{1}(X/B)} \to \Aut(W)$ a homomorphism of sheaves of groups. Then there exists a unique $f$-local system $M$ such that $\rho_{M} = \rho$.
