Definition 2.3. (4707)

An optimal sequence of generators for $\pi_1\Sigma$ is a sequence of loops $(a_1,b_1,\cdots,a_g,b_g,c_1,\cdots,c_n)$ obtained from a standard presentation of $\pi_1\Sigma$ as above.
