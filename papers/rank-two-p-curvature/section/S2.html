Section 2. Rank two local systems on surfaces

The primary goal of this section is to prove Theorems 1.3 [5AC4] and 1.4 [BBD3], which will be used in the proof of Theorem 1.2 [E65B]. We begin by establishing, in Sections 2.1 and 2.2, nonarchimedean and archimedean boundedness properties (corresponding to points (1) and (2) provided by the summary in Section 1.2) for certain representations of surface groups into $\SL_2(\C)$. In Section 2.3, we use these results to prove Theorems 1.3 [5AC4] and 1.4 [BBD3], as outlined in Section 1.2. Finally, we deduce Theorem 1.2 [E65B], admitting in advance Theorem 1.5 [6BC3] (proved in later sections) in the genus zero case.
