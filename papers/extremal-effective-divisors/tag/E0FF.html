Proof. (E0FF)

By Lemma 4.2 [A447], $B\cdot \pi^{*}BN^1_d < 0$ and it is not entirely contained in the boundary of $\BM_{1,4d-4}$, hence $B\cdot \wt{BN}^1_d < 0$. Moreover, by the construction of $B$, it is a moving curve in $\wt{BN}^1_d$. It follows that $\wt{BN}^1_d$ spans an extremal ray of $\BEff(\BM_{1,4d-4})$. The same argument as in the proof of Theorem 3.3 [ED28] shows that $\wt{BN}^1_d$ and $D_a$ span different extremal rays of $\BEff(\BM_{1,4d-4})$.
