Theorem 3.3. (ED28)

The divisor $\wt{BN}^1_3$ is an extremal divisor on $\BM_{1,8}$. For every $n\geq 8$, $f^{*}\wt{BN^1_8}$ spans an extremal ray of $\BEff(\BM_{1,n})$ that is different from the ray of $D_a$.
