Lemma 5.2. (6CFF)

The following intersection numbers hold in the Chow ring of $X$: $$\begin{aligned}& f_{i}^{2} = f_{2} \cdot e_{i} = \Sigma \cdot \Pi = \Sigma \cdot e_{i} = 0, \\ & \Pi^{3} = \Sigma^{3} = 4, \\ & e_{i}^{3} = -1, \\ & e_{i}^2 \cdot f_{1} = e_{i}^2 \cdot \Pi = -1, \\ & \Sigma^2 \cdot f_{1} = -\Pi^2 \cdot f_{1} = -2, \\ & \Sigma^2 \cdot f_{2} = -\Pi^2 \cdot f_{2} = -1, \\ & \Sigma \cdot f_{1} \cdot f_{2} = \Pi \cdot f_{1} \cdot f_{2} = 1.\end{aligned}$$

Furthermore, let $l_{i}$ and $r_{i}$ be the line and ruling classes of $E_{i} \cong \bbF_{1}$. Then $E_{i}\cdot {E_{i}} = r_{i} - l_{i}$ and $\Pi \cdot E_{i} = f_{1} \cdot E_{i} = r_{i}$.
