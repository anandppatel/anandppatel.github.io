Proposition 2.2. (EF94)

Let \(s \in \Hom(V, W)\) be an injective homomorphism with image \(S\). Let \(\Gamma \subset \overline H\) be the stabiliser of \([S] \in \Gr(r+1, W)\) and assume that it is finite. Let \(\phi \colon X \to \Gr(r+1, W)\) be a complete parametrisation of the \(\overline H\)-orbit of \([S]\). Then, in \(A^*_G(\Hom(V,W)) = A^*_G(\bullet)\), we have $$|\Gamma|[\Orb(s)] = \int_X c_{\rm top} \Hom(\underline V, \phi^*Q).$$
