Proposition 2.1. (AFBB)

Let \(w \in W\) be non-zero. Let \(\Gamma \subset \overline G\) be the stabiliser of \([w] \in \P W\) and assume that it is finite. Let \(\phi \colon X \to \P W\) be a complete parametrisation of the \(\overline G\) orbit of \([w] \in \P W\). Then, in \(A^*_G(W) = A^*_G(\bullet)\), we have $$|\Gamma| [\Orb(w)] = \int_X c_{\rm top}(\phi^* Q).$$
