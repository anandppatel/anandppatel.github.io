Theorem 1.4. (7440)

The orbit closure \(\Orb(S) \subset \Gr(r+1, \Sym^d\k^2)\) is a union of finitely many orbits. In addition to the orbit of \(S\), these are the orbits of subspaces of the form $$\langle w_1^{d-a_0}w_2^{a_0}, \dots, w_1^{d-a_r}w_2^{a_r} \rangle,$$ where \(a_0 < \dots < a_r\) is the vanishing sequence of \(S\) at some point of \(\P^1\) and \(w_1, w_2\) is a basis of \(\k^2\).
