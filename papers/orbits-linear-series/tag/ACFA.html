Proof. (ACFA)

Consider the family \(m_t\) as in (4.4), where \([v_2] = b\). Then, in \(\widetilde \P\) we have $$\lim_{t \to 0} m_t = p.$$ Therefore, we have $$ \widetilde S|_p = \lim_{t \to 0} m_t(S).$$ Proposition 4.3 [38C5] gives the limit and yields $$ \widetilde S|_p = \langle w_1^{d-a_0}w_2^{a_0}, \dots, w_1^{d-a_r}w_2^{a_r}\rangle.$$ The statement follows.
