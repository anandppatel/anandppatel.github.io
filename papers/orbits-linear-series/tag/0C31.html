Remark 1.2. (0C31)

Although Theorem 1.1 [C88F] gives the non-equivariant classes by the series of substitutions mentioned above, it does not give a simple expression for it. Indeed, the substitution \(\omega_1 = \omega_2 = 0\) can only be performed after dividing by the denominator in Theorem 1.1 [C88F], and the result of the division is not obvious.
