Proposition 2.13. (96E0)

Let $Z \subset \P^2$ be an admissible, length $6$ subscheme and $X_Z \subset \P^3$ its associated cubic surface. The map $$ \Aut(\P^2, Z) \to \Aut(X_Z)$$ is injective and its image has finite index. In particular, $\Aut(\P^2, Z)$ is finite if and only if $\Aut(X_Z)$ is.
