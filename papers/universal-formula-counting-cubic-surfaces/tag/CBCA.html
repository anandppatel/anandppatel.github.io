Corollary 1.4. (CBCA)

Let $\pi \colon \cX \to B$ is a good family of cubic surfaces parametrized by a $4$ dimensional proper variety $B$. Assume that a general fiber of $\pi$ is smooth and the induced map $\mu \colon B \dashrightarrow \mathcal M$ is dominant. Then the degree of $\mu$ is divisible by 1080.
