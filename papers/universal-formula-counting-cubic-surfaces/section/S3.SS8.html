3.8. The family $A_4 + A_1$

Consider $$X = V(x_3(x_0x_2-x_1^2) - x_0^2x_1).$$ A $\G_m$ stabilizing $X$ acts on the variables by weights $(1,-1,-3,3)$. The weight $w$ of the cubic defining $X$ is $1$. From (3.28), we get $$\pi_*\left( \omega_X^{-1} \right) = \chi(2) \oplus \chi(0) \oplus \chi(-2) \oplus \chi(4).$$ Setting $q = c_1(\chi(1)) \in A^1(B\G_m)$, we get $$v_1 = 4q, \quad v_2 = -4q^2, \quad v_3 = -16q^3, \quad v_4 = 0,$$ and hence $$v_1^4 = 256q^4 \quad v_1^2v_2 = -64q^4, \quad v_1v_3 = -64q^4, \quad v_2^2 = 16q^4, \quad v_4 = 0.$$ Substituting in (3.27) yields the relation $$16a_{1^4} -4a_{1^2 2}- 4a_{13} + a_{2^2} = 0.$$
