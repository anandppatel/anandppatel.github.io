3.9. The family $D_4$

Consider $$X = V(x_3x_0^2 - x_1^3-x_2^3).$$ A $\G_m$ stabilizing $X$ acts on the variables by weights $(5,1,1,-7)$. The weight $w$ of the cubic equation defining $X$ is $3$. By (3.28), we get $$\pi_*\left( \omega_X^{-1} \right) = \chi(8) \oplus \chi(4) \oplus \chi(4) \oplus \chi(-4).$$ Setting $q = c_1(\chi(1))$, we get $$v_1 = 12q, \quad v_2 = 16q^2, \quad v_3 = -192q^3, \quad v_4 = -512q^4,$$ and hence $$v_1^4 = 20736q^4 \quad v_1^2v_2 = 2304q^4, \quad v_1v_3 = -2304q^4, \quad v_2^2 = 256q^4, \quad v_4 = -512q^4.$$ Substituting in (3.27) yields the relation $$81a_{1^4} +9a_{1^2 2}- 9a_{13} + a_{2^2}-2a_4 = 0.$$
