Section 2. Preliminaries

In this chapter, we introduce the notion of a good family of cubic surfaces (Definition 2.4 [5026]) and provide a tool (Proposition 2.13 [96E0]) to verify that a family is good. Before we do so, we first establish notation and recall some facts about cubic surfaces and their moduli.
