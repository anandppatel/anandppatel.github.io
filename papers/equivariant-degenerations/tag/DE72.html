Lemma 8.13. (DE72)

Maintain the setting in the discussion immediately prior. If $$\gamma: U^{\times} \to PGL(V)$$ is the $1$-parameter family defined by $$\begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & t^3 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & t^4\\ \end{pmatrix}$$ then $\gamma$ is full for $\alpha_{\on{h.flex}}$ and $\alpha_{\on{h.flex}}^{\gamma}(0)$ is a curve projectively equivalent to $C_{E_{6}}$.
