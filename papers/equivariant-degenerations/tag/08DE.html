Theorem 8.17. (08DE)

Let $C$ be a smooth quartic plane curve with $n$ hyperflexes. Then, $$\begin{aligned}\p_C = 8\p_{C_{A_6}}-n\p_{C_{E_6}}.\end{aligned}$$
