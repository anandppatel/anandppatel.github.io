Remark 1.3. (72B2)

It is tempting to apply Kazarian's work on multisingularities [Kaz03b] to the locus of curves with an $A_5$ and an $A_1$ singularity to find $p_{C_{AN}}$ in Figure 1. However, in addition to $O_{C_{AN}}$, there is another component of the locus of quartics with an $A_5$ and an $A_1$ singularity, namely two conics meeting at two points with multiplicities 3 and 1 respectively. Therefore, it was necessary to compute $O_{C_{AN}}$ independently (see Section 7).
