Proof. (469C)

The map $\varphi': \mathcal{C}' \to \P^{2}$ from (7.1) resolves the composite $$\mathcal{C} \to \P V \dashrightarrow \P V$$ where the first map is $\varphi$ and the second rational map sends a point $[x:y:z]$ to $[x:t^3y:t^4z]$. Therefore, with $\gamma$ as stated in the lemma, the fact that $\overline{E} = C_{E_{6}}$ completes the proof.
