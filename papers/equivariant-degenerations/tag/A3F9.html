Definition 2.14. (A3F9)

Define $$\begin{aligned}\mathsf{Inv}(V) \subset \P \on{End}(V) \times \P \on{End}(V)\end{aligned}$$ to be the closure of the graph of the inversion morphism $i: PGL(V) \to PGL(V)$. In other words, $\mathsf{Inv}(V)$ is the closure of the set of pairs $(g,g^{-1})$, where $g \in PGL(V)$.
