Lemma 10.1. (C4F5)

Let $Z\subset Y$ be an inclusion of smooth varieties. Let $W\subset Y$ be a smooth subvariety and $\wt{W}\subset {\rm Bl}_Z Y$ be the proper transform of $W$. If $\pi: {\rm Bl}_Z Y\to Y$ is the blowup map, then we have the short exact sequence $$\begin{aligned}0\to \on{coker}(\pi^{*}N_{W/Y}^\vee \to N_{\wt{W}/{\rm Bl}_Z Y}^\vee)\to \Omega_{{\rm Bl}_Z Y/Y}|_{\wt{W}}\to \Omega_{\wt{W}/W}\to 0.\end{aligned}$$
