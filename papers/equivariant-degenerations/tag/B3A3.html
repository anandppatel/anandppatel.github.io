Proposition 9.2. (B3A3)

The constant term of the equivariant class of the $GL_2$-orbit of $d$ points on $\mb{P}^1$ with multiplicities $m_1,\ldots,m_n$ so $\sum_{i=1}^{n}{m_i}=d$ is $$\begin{aligned}\frac{1}{(v-u)^2}\prod_{i=0}^{d}{(iu+(n-i)v)}\left(\frac{n-2}{duv}-\sum_{i=1}^{n}{\frac{-2m_i+d}{((d-m_i)u+m_iv)((d-m_i)v+m_iu)}}\right).\end{aligned}$$ To get the entire class, one substitutes $t_i+\frac{H}{n}$ for each $t_i$.
