Definition 6.1. (FA95)

Define the curves $C_{BN}$, $C_{AN}$, $C_{\on{flex}}[j]$, $j \in \k$ as:

  1. $C_{BN}: Z^{d-3}(XYZ + X^3 + Z^3) = 0$\\
  2. $C_{AN}: Z^{d-3}(Y^2Z - X^3 + X^2Z) = 0$\\
  3. $C_{\on{flex}}[j]$: This is the union of a smooth cubic curve with $j$-invariant $j$ along with one of its flex lines, the line taken with multiplicity $(d-3)$.
