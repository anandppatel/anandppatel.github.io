Remark 7.15. (FE4C)

Condition (4) imposed on our family $\alpha_{\on{h.flex}}$ is not strictly necessary. Possibly after performing a base change, and then blowing up $\mathcal{C}$ iteratively until the sections $\sigma_{i}$ become separated, we arrive at a specialization to the curve $C_{E_{6}}$. We omitted this for brevity.
