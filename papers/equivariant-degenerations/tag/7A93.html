Lemma 8.11. (7A93)

Let $C \subset\mb{P}^2$ be a rational quartic possessing one $E_6$ singularity and two distinct flex points. Then, $C$ has a full dimensional orbit, and is therefore projectively equivalent to $C_{E_6}$.
