Definition 2.1. (EFED)

Given $Z$ as above, define $[Z]_{GL(V)}$ to be the polynomial in $c_1,\ldots,c_{r+1}$ such that the class of $Z_{\mc{V}}$ is $[Z]_{GL(V)}$ with the chern classes of $\mc{V}$ substituted for $c_1,\ldots,c_{r+1}$. Equivalently, $[Z]_{GL(V)}$ is the $GL(V)$-equivariant class of $Z$ in $A_{GL(V)}^{\bullet}(\on{Sym}^dV^{\vee})\cong \mb{Z}[c_1,\ldots,c_{r+1}]$.
