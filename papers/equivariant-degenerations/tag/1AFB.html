Corollary 8.2. (1AFB)

In the setting of Theorem 8.1 [3F96] if each $m_i=1$, then $n=d$, and $$\begin{aligned}\mathsf{P}_X(-u,-v) = n(n-1)(n-2)\prod_{j=2}^{n-2}{(H+(ju+(n-j)v))}.\end{aligned}$$
