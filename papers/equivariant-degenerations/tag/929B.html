Proposition 6.2. (929B)

Keep the setting above. For every $j \in \k$, $$\# \on{Aut}(C_{\on{flex}}[j]) \cdot [\P \Orb(C_{\on{flex}}[j])] \leadsto \#\on{Aut}(C_{AN})[\P \Orb(C_{AN})] + \#\on{Aut}(C_{BN})[\P \Orb(C_{BN})]$$ and therefore $$\p_{C_{\on{flex}}[j]} = \p_{C_{AN}} + 2\p_{C_{BN}}.$$
