Lemma 6.11. (A238)

Maintain the setting above, and let $\gamma(t)$ denote the family of matrices $$\begin{pmatrix} t^{-1/3} & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & t^{-1/2} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1\\ \end{pmatrix}$$ Then the curve $\alpha_{\on{cusp}}^{\gamma}(0)$ is defined by the equation $$\begin{aligned}\lim_{t\to 0}t^{-1}(F(t^{\frac{1}{3}}X,t^{\frac{1}{2}}Y,Z))\end{aligned}$$ and is projectively equivalent to the curve $C_{\on{flex}}[0]: Z^{d-3}(X^3+Y^2Z+Z^3) = 0$. (See Definition 6.1 [FA95].)
