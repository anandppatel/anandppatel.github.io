Definition 2.6. (69D0)

The predegree of a hypersurface $X \subset \P V$ having full dimensional orbit is $\#\on{Aut}(X)$ times the degree of the orbit closure $\P \Orb(X)$ in the projective space $\mb{P} \on{Sym}^{d}V^{\vee}$. If the orbit of $X$ is not full dimensional then we define its predegree to be zero.
