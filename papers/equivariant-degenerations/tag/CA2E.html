Remark 2.13. (CA2E)

Observe that the condition ``$\gamma_{1}(0)$ and $\gamma_{2}(0)$ are not $PGL(V)$ translates'' is equivalent to the condition that the projective endomorphisms $\gamma_{i}(0)$ do not have the same kernel subspaces in $\P V$ in the case of the action by post-composition. We will use this observation repeatedly.
