Definition 2.10. (67EE)

Suppose $\gamma$ is full for $\alpha$. Define $$\mathcal{A}_{\gamma} \subset \mathcal{Y}_{0}$$ to be the closure of the orbit of $(0, \gamma(0), \alpha^{\gamma}(0))$.
