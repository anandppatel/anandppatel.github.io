Section 7. Degenerations III: Quartic Plane Curves

In this section, we finally specialize all the way to the setting $r=2, d=4$ of quartic curves. We thoroughly study the classical degeneration to a double conic. Then we investigate the effect of acquiring a hyperflex on $\p_{C}$. (We believe the hyperflex specialization can be analyzed in arbitrary degree, but the algorithm in [AF00b] is too complicated for us to apply with sufficient confidence.)
