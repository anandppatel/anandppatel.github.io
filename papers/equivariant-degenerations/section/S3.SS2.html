3.2. Simplifying notation in the case $r=2$.

From here onward, we will specialize to the case of plane curves: $V$ is a $3$-dimensional vector space. We let $X,Y,Z \in V^{\vee}$ be homogeneous coordinates on $\P V$. In terms of this choice, we write: \begin{itemize} \item $PGL_3$ instead of $PGL(V)$, \item $\P^8$ instead of $\P \on{End}(V)$, and

\item $\P^{{d+2 \choose 2}-1}$ instead of $\P \on{Sym}^{d}V^{\vee}$. \end{itemize} \end{comment}
