Section 9. Points on $\mathbb{P}^1$ via Atiyah-Bott

\label{AB} The method in Section 8 was closer to the theme of equivariant degeneration explored in this paper. We note that there is self-contained proof given by the Atiyah-Bott formula, or equivalently resolution and integral via localization [FR06, Section 4]. The authors attempted to perform the same method for smooth plane curves using the resolution given by [AF93b], but the computation of the normal bundles quickly became intractable.
