Remark 2.2. (8605)

  1. We will use Lemma 2.1 in a situation where $Y$ is a Grassmannian: every non-constant morphism out of a Grassmannian is necessarily finite (because the Grassmannian's Picard group is $\Z$), and so the critical hypothesis of Lemma 2.1 will be satisfied.
  2. Lemma 2.1 is true in arbitrary characteristic.
