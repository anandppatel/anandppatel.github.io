Theorem 4.7. (0E20)

Let $m, n$ be two positive integers satisfying $m \leq n$ and consider the variety of first transvectants $\beta: \transvectant_{m,n} \to \P S_{m} \times \P S_{n} \times \P S_{m+n-2}$. For $i \in \{1,2,3\}$ let $\zeta_{i} \in \CH^{1}(\P S_{m} \times \P S_{n} \times \P S_{m+n-2})$ denote the pullback of the hyperplane class from the $i$-th factor.

  1. If $\gcd(m,n) = 1$, then $$ \beta_{*}[\transvectant_{m,n}] = \left[\frac{(1+\zeta_{1}+\zeta_{2})^{m+n-1}}{1+\zeta_{1}+\zeta_{2}-\zeta_{3}} \right]_{m+n-2}. $$
  2. If $\gcd(m,n) = 2$, then $$\begin{aligned}\beta_{*}[\transvectant_{m,n}] & = \left[\frac{(1+\zeta_{1}+\zeta_{2})^{m+n-1}}{1+\zeta_{1}+\zeta_{2}-\zeta_{3}} \right]_{m+n-2} \\ & - 2^{m-2}\left(\left(\frac{m}{2}\right)^{2}\zeta_{1}^{m-2}\zeta_{2}^{n} + \frac{n}{2}\frac{m}{2}\zeta_{1}^{n-1}\zeta_{2}^{m-1} + \left(\frac{n}{2}\right)^{2}\zeta_{1}^{m}\zeta_{2}^{n-2}\right).\end{aligned}$$
