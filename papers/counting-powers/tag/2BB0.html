Corollary 4.6. (2BB0)

Let $\alpha_{1}$ and $\alpha_2$ denote the classes found in Corollary 4.5. Write $\gamma \in \CH^{m+n} \P S_{m} \times \P S_{n} \times \P S_{m+n-2}$ for the degree $m+n$ term of the formal sum $\sum_{i=0}^{\infty} (-\alpha_{1}-\alpha_{2})^{i}$.

Then the locus of forms in $\P S_{d}$ which are the sum of an $a$'th power and a $b$'th power is the intersection product $$\int_{\P S_{m} \times \P S_{n} \times \P S_{m+n-2}} \gamma \cap \beta_{*}[\transvectant_{m,n}].$$
