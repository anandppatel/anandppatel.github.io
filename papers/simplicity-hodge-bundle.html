The Simplicity of the Hodge Bundle

Anand Patel
arXiv · Accepted for publication in Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences, (2026).

Commentary

This paper proves that the Hodge bundle $\Omega_g$ over $\mathscr{M}_g$ is simple—it contains no non-trivial sub-bundles. The argument uses families of curves with $(\\mathbb{Z}/2\\mathbb{Z})^2$ symmetry to constrain the trace function of any putative sub-bundle, ultimately showing it must have rank $0$ or $g$.
