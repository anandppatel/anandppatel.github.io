Theorem 4.1. (58D6)

Let $C$ be a reduced plane curve of degree $d$. Then, the map $$\begin{aligned}\Phi: \operatorname{Mor}_e(\mathbb{P}^1,\mathbb{P}^2)&\dashrightarrow \mathbb{P}(H^0(\OO_{\PP^1}(ed)))\\ [\iota]&\mapsto [\iota^{-1}(C)]\end{aligned}$$ has maximal rank if $C$ has finite stabilizer under the action of $PGL_{3}$.
