Corollary 6.6. (9754)

We have the following closed-form expressions for $B_k(n)$ for $k=2,3,4,5$. For $k=2$ and $n\geq 0$ $$\begin{aligned}B_2(3n+1)&=\frac{3n^2+n}{2} \quad B_2(3n+2)=\frac{3n^2+3n}{2} \quad B_2(3n+3)=\frac{3n^2+5n+2}{2}.\end{aligned}$$ For $k=3$, $$\begin{aligned}B_3(7n+1)&=\frac{7n^2+n}{2}\quad B_3(7n+2)=\frac{7n^2+3n}{2}\quad B_3(7n+3)=\frac{7n^2+5n}{2}\\ B_3(7n+4)&=\frac{7n^2+7n+2}{2}\quad B_3(7n+5)=\frac{7n^2+9n+2}{2}\quad
