Corollary 2.26. (BC02)

Let $X \subset \P^{r}$ be a general degree $d \geq 2r-1$ hypersurface, and let $k = d-(2r-2)$. Then $$\begin{aligned}\left( \Sigma \subset \P \to \G\left( 1,r \right) \right)\end{aligned}$$ is versal at every $k$-incident line $\left[ \ell \right] \in \G\left( 1,r \right)$.
