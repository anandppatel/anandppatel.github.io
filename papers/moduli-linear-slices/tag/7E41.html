Theorem 4.18. (7E41)

Suppose $d \geq 2r+2$, and $X \subset \P^{r}$ is a general degree $d$ hypersurface. Then the moduli map $$\begin{aligned}\mu_{1} : \G\left( 1,r \right) \dashrightarrow \Hyp(d,1)\end{aligned}$$ is generically injective.
