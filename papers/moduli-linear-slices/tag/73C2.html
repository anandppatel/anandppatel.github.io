Theorem 4.24. (73C2)

For any multiplicity $(a,b,c)$, let $n_{a,b,c}$ denote the number of $3$-incident lines to $X$ having the given multiplicity. Then the degree of the map $\alpha: Z \to \barm_{0,d}$ is $$\begin{aligned}\deg \alpha = 2 \sum_{a \geq b > 1}^{}n_{(a,b,1)} + 4n_{d-2,1,1}.\end{aligned}$$

Furthermore, $\deg \alpha = \deg \mu_{1}$.
