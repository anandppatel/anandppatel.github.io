Lemma 2.23. (D46C)

Maintain the setup before the previous lemma. Then there exists an $r-1$-dimensional subspace $V_{r-1} \subset P_{d-1}$ such that the multiplication-then-restriction map $$\begin{aligned}\rho': P_{1}\otimes V_{r-1} \to k[z]/(z-\alpha_{1})^{n_{1}} \oplus_{i=2}^{k}k[z]/(z-\alpha_{i})^{n_{i}-1}\end{aligned}$$ has the following properties:

  1. $\rho'$ is injective.
  2. The image of $\rho'$ does not contain the element $$\begin{aligned}w = ((z-\alpha_{1})^{n_{1}-1},0,0,\dots).\end{aligned}$$
