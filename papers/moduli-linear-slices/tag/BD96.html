Definition 2.4. (BD96)

We let $W_{n}$, $(n > 1)$, denote the pair $(Y_{n},\P^{1} \times \A^{n-1} \to (\A^{n-1},0))$, with $Y_{n} \subset \P^{1} \times \A^{n-1}$ defined by the equation $$\begin{aligned}s^{n} + a_{n-2}s^{n-2}t^{2} + a_{n-3}s^{n-3}t^{3} + \dots + a_{0}t^{n} = 0,\end{aligned}$$ where $a_{i}$ are coordinates on $\A^{n-1}$, and $[s:t]$ are homogeneous coordinates on $\P^{1}$.
