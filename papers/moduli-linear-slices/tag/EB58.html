Proof. (EB58)

If $T_{\PP^2}(-\log C)|_{\ell}$ has a section, then it must split as $\OO_{\ell}(k) \oplus \OO_{\ell}(3-d-k)$ for some $k \geq 0$. Since $d\geq 5$, we can apply 3.1 [6C21] to see there is a rank 1 subsheaf of $T_{\PP^2}(-\log C)$ of degree $k$. Taking its reflexive hull, we find a line subbundle of $T_{\PP^2}(-\log C)$ of degree $k$, which must be $\OO_{\PP^2}(k)$, which has a section.
