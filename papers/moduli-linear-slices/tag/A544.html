Theorem 4.2. (A544)

Furthermore we get a complete classification of cases when $\Phi$ in Theorem 4.1 [58D6] does not have maximal rank:

  1. $d\geq 5$: $C$ is a union of orbits under an action of $\mathbb{G}_m$ or $\mathbb{G}_a$ on $\PP^2$
  2. $d=4$:
    1. $e=1$ and $C$ is the union of four concurrent lines
    2. $e\geq 2$ and $C$ is a union of orbits under an action of $\mathbb{G}_m$ or $\mathbb{G}_a$ on $\PP^2$
  3. $d=3$: $e\geq 2$ and $C$ is union of concurrent lines.
