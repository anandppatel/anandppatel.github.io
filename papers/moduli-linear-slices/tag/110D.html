Proposition 2.7. (110D)

Let $(B.b)$ be as in the previous proposition, but now suppose $Z \subset \P^{1} \times B$ is a divisor whose restriction to $Z_{b} \subset \P^{1} \times \left\{ b \right\}$ is of the form $n_{1}p_{1} + \dots + n_{i}p_{i}$ with each $n_{j} \geq 1$.

Then there exists an étale open neighborhood $U$ of $b$ such that $Z_{U}$ is a disjoint union $ \coprod_{j}Z_{j} $, and furthermore there exists a map $(U,b) \to \left( \prod_{j}\A^{n_{j}-1},0 \right)$ such that $Z_{j}$ is isomorphic to the pullback of $W_{n_{j}}$ under the composite $(U,b) \to \left(\prod_{j}\A^{n_{j}-1},0 \right) \to \left(\A^{n_{j}-1},0\right)$.
