Remark 4.12. (CE30)

The process of stable reduction described in the proof of Proposition 4.11 [86A6] will be referred to frequently in the remainder of this section. We emphasize that when $k \geq 3$, the original fiber $\P^{1}_{b}$ naturally persists as a component of the stable reduction $P$.
