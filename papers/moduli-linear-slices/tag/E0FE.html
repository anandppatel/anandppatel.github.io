Theorem 5.6. (E0FE)

Let $X$ be a smooth, degree $d$ hypersurface in $\PP^n$ with $d>B_k(n)+n+1$. Then, $$\begin{aligned}\Phi: \operatorname{Mor}_1(\PP^k,\PP^n)&\dashrightarrow \PP H^0(\PP^k,\OO_{\PP^k}(d))\\ \iota &\mapsto [\iota^{-1}(X)].\end{aligned}$$ is of maximal rank.
