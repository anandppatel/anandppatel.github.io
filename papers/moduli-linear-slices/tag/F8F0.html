Proof. (F8F0)

By definition, we have the short exact sequence $$\begin{aligned}0\to T_{Y\times\cM/Z}|_{Y\times\{[f]\}}\to T_{Y\times\cM/Z}|_{Y\times\{[f]\}}\to f^{*}T_Z\to 0.\end{aligned}$$ We have the natural decomposition $T_{Y\times\cM/Z}|_{Y\times\{[f]\}}\cong H^0(f^{*}T_Z)\otimes \cO\oplus T_Y$, with respect to which the natural map $T_{Y\times\cM/Z}|_{Y\times\{[f]\}}\to f^{*}T_Z$ is $\operatorname{ev}+df$. Consider the following commutative diagram, where $K$ is the Lazarsfeld-Mukai bundle of $f^{*}T_Z$:
