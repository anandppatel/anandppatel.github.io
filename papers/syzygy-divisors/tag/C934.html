Definition 2.6. (C934)

Suppose $d$ divides $g-1$. Define the $i$th syzygy divisor $\mu_i \subset \widetilde H_{d,g}$ as the locus of $\alpha \from C \to \P^1$ for which the bundle $N_i$ is unbalanced.
