Theorem 1.1. (B6A0)

Suppose $d$ divides $g-1$. Let $i$ be an integer with $1 \leq i \leq d-3$. The locus $\mu_i \subset \widetilde H_{d,g}$ is an effective divisor whose class in $\Pic_\Q\widetilde H_{d,g}$ is given by $$[\mu_i] = A_i \left(6(gd-6g+d+6) \cdot \zeta - d(d-12)\cdot \kappa - d^2 \cdot \delta \right),$$ where $$A_i = {{d-4} \choose {i-1}}^2 \frac{(d-2)(d-3)}{6(i+1)(d-i-1)}.$$
