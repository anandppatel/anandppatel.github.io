Section 2. Divisors on Hurwitz spaces

The goal of this section is to recall the rational Picard group of the Hurwitz space and some divisor classes in it. Since we are only interested in the rational Picard group, we may work either with the Deligne–Mumford stack $\widetilde{\orb H}_{d,g}$ or the coarse moduli space $\widetilde H_{d,g}$. We will pass from one to the other without comment.
