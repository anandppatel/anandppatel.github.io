Proposition 3.4. (78BF)

For a family $f \from \cC \to S$ as above, $$24(b-1)\lambda - 3(b-2)\delta + 6\sfD - (b-10)\sfT = 0$$ where $b = 2g+2d-2$ is the number of branch points.
