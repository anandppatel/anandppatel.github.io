Lemma 1.12. (B34B)
  1. Let $\alpha \colon R \to \P^1$ and $\beta \from X \to \P^1$ be degree $d$ covers where $R$ is a smooth rational curve and $X$ is a smooth elliptic curve. Then the following hold:
    1. $\cE_{\alpha} = \cO(1)^{\oplus d-1}$
    2. $\cF_{\alpha} = \cO(1)^{\oplus d-3} \oplus \cO(2)^{\oplus {d-2 \choose 2}}$
    3. $\cE_{\beta} = \cO(1)^{\oplus d-2} \oplus \cO(2)$
    4. $\cF_{\beta} = \cO(2)^{\oplus \frac{d(d-3)}{2}}$
  2. Let $\gamma \from C \to \P^1$ be a general degree $d \leq 5$ cover, with $g(C) \leq 4$. Then $\cF_{\gamma}$ is balanced.
