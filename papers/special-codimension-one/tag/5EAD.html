Corollary 3.6. (5EAD)

Let $d \geq 4.$ Then the divisor classes $\sfM$ and $\sfCE$ and boundary divisors of $ø{\H}_{d,g}$ are linearly independent. The same is true when $d=3$ when we omit $\sfCE$ (which is zero).
