6.1. Rational normal curves

Let $X \subset \P^n$ be a rational normal curve. Plainly, $X$ is incompressible, and hence the projection-ramification map $$ \rho \from \Gr(2, n+1) \to \P^{2n-2}$$ is a regular map. Therefore, we get $$\begin{aligned}\deg \rho &= c_1(\rho^* \O(1))^{2n-2}\\ &= c_1(\O_{\Gr(r+1, n+1)}(1))^{2n-2} \\ &= \frac{(2n-2)!}{n!(n-1)!}.\end{aligned}$$
