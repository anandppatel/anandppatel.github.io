Theorem 5.7. (9955)

{oss:14}}]

The functor that associates to a $B$-scheme $S \to B$ the set of linked linear series on $\mathcal E_S$ is representable by a projective $B$-scheme $\mathcal G(k, \mathcal E)$ isomorphic to the Grassmannian $\Gr(k, H^0(X_\eta, \mathcal E_\eta))$ over $\eta$. The locus of simple linear series ${\mathcal G}^{\rm simple}(k, \mathcal E) \subset {\mathcal G}(k, \mathcal E)$ is an open subscheme, and the map ${\mathcal G}^{\rm simple}(k, \mathcal E) \to B$ has universal relative dimension at least $k(d-k-r(g-1))$.
