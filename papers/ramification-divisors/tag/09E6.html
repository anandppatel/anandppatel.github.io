Theorem 5.16. (09E6)

Let $E$ be a generic vector bundle on $\P^1$ of rank $r$ and degree $d = a(r-1) + b(2r-1)+1$, where $a, b$ are positive integers. Then the projection-ramification map is generically finite, and hence dominant, for $E$. In particular, the projection-ramification map is dominant for generic $E$ of degree $\geq (r-1)(2r-1)+1$.
