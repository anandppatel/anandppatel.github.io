Theorem 4.2. (DAB3)

Let $X \subset \P^n$ be a smooth, non-degenerate projective variety of dimension $r \geq 1$ over a field of characteristic zero. We have the inequality $$\dim \Gr(n-r, n+1) \leq \dim |K_X + (r+1)H|,$$ where equality holds if and only if $X$ is a variety of minimal degree, that is $\deg X = n-r+1$.
