Theorem 1.3. (7F28)

Let $X \subset \P^{n}$ be a smooth, non-degenerate projective variety of dimension $r$ over a field of characteristic zero. We have the inequality $$ \dim \Gr(n-r, n+1) \leq \dim |K_X + (r+1)H|.$$ Equality holds if and only if $X$ is a variety of minimal degree, that is $\deg X = n-r+1$.
