Remark 3.11. (FD0B)

In the proof of Proposition 3.10 [14AC], we showed that $$N_{Z^+/\widetilde{P}^+} \big|_{Z} \to N_{\delta_y/X} \otimes N_{\delta_y/R_y}$$ is surjective on global sections. Again, this is stronger than what was required for Proposition 3.10 [14AC]; it will be useful later.
