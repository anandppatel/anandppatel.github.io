Proposition 4.4. (9111)

Let $E$ be a vector bundle of rank $(d-1)$ on $\P^{r}$, where $r \geq d+1$. Then $E(n)$ is not a Tschirnhausen bundle of a smooth, connected cover for sufficiently large $n$.
