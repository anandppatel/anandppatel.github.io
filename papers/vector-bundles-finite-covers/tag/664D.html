Remark 3.9. (664D)

From the surjection (3.20), we observe that the map $$\nu_* \nu^* N_{Z/\widetilde{P}} \to \nu_*\left(\nu^* N_{Z/\widetilde{P}}\big|_\Gamma \right)\oplus N_{Z/\widetilde{P}}\big|_{\delta_S},$$ is surjective on global sections. This is stronger than what was required for Proposition 3.8 [3AC7]; it will be useful later.
