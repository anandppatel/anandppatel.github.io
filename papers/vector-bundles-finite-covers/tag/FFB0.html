Proposition 3.1. (FFB0)

Let $E = L_1 \oplus \dots \oplus L_{d-1}$, where the $L_i$ are line bundles on $Y$ with $\deg L_1 \geq 2g_Y-1$ and $\deg L_{i+1} \geq \deg L_i + (2g_Y-1)$ for $i \in \{1, \dots, d-2\}$. There exists a nodal curve $X$ and a finite flat map $\phi \from X \to Y$ of degree $d$ such that $E_\phi \cong E$.
