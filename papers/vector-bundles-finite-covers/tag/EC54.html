Proposition 3.4. (EC54)

The restriction of $N_{Z/\widetilde{P}}$ to $R_y$ is isomorphic to $\O(d+1)^{d-2} \oplus \O(1)$, where the sub-bundle $\O(d+1)^{d-2}$ is the image of the natural map $$ N_{R_y/P_y} \to N_{Z/\widetilde{P}}\big|_{R_y},$$ and the quotient $\O(1)$ is an inflation of $N_{P_y/\widetilde{P}}\big |_{R_y}$ at the points of $\delta_y$.
