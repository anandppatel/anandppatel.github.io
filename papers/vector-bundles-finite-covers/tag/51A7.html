Proposition 3.3. (51A7)

Let $X$ be a nodal curve and $X \to Y$ a finite flat morphism with Tschirnhausen bundle $E$. For some line bundle $L$ on $Y$, the following holds. There exists a smooth curve $X'$ and a finite morphism $X' \to Y$ such that

  1. The Tschirnhausen bundle of $X' \to Y$ is $E' = E \otimes L$.
  2. We have $H^1(X', N_{X'/ E'}) = 0$, where $X' \subset E'$ is the canonical affine embedding.

Furthermore, there exists an $n$ (depending on $X \to Y$), such that the above holds for any $L$ of degree at least $n$.
