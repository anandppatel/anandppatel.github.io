Proof. (3141)

Extend $E$ to a vector bundle $E'$ on the smooth projective compactification $Y'$ of $Y$. Apply Theorem 1.1 [7AFF] to $E'$, twisting by a sufficiently positive line bundle $L$ on $Y'$ whose divisor class is supported on the complement $Y' \setminus Y$. We obtain a smooth curve $X'$ and a map $\phi \from X' \to Y'$ whose Tschirnhausen bundle is $E' \otimes L$; letting $X = \phi^{-1}(Y)$, we obtain the corollary.
