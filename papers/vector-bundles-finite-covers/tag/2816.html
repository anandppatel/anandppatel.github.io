Proof of Proposition 3.6 [3FA3]. (2816)

By Lemma 3.7 [ACA8] and Proposition 2.5 [2285], if $y \in Y$ and $R_y$ are general, then the inflation of $\phi_*M$ at $y$ given by $K$ leads to a non-zero decrease in $h^1$. As a result, if $|S| \geq h^1(M)$, and $S \subset Y$ and $R_y$ are general, then $H^1(K) = 0$.
