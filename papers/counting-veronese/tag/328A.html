Corollary 3.18. (328A)

The space of complete triangles $\CT$ is the closure (with reduced, induced scheme structure) of the graph of the triangle map $$ \tau \from \Hilb_{3}\P^{2} \dashrightarrow \Hilb_{3}\check{\P}^{2} $$ which assigns to a set of three distinct, non-collinear points $T \subset \P^{2}$, the set of lines spanned by pairs in $T$.
