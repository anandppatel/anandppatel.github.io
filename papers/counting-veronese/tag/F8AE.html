Proof of Theorem 1.2 [61DC]. (F8AE)

By Theorem 4.25 [2247] our task is to compute $$\int_{\SQP} [\Dom(p)]^{13}.$$ We will do so by instead computing $$\int_{\CT}\varphi_{*}\left([\Dom(p)]^{13}\right),$$ which by Theorem 4.12 [FAF5] equals $$\int_{\CT}\varphi_{*}\left(([\BP(p)] - 4[\Inc(p)] - [\Lin(p)])^{13}\right).$$ Using the push-pull formula for $\varphi$ and Lemma 5.2 [EBB7], we must then compute: $$\begin{aligned}\int_{\CT} \sum_{i=0}^{3} (-1)^{i}{13 \choose i} \cdot \Delta_{2i} \cdot \left( 4 [\inc(p)]+[\lin(p)]\right)^{i}.\end{aligned}$$

By expressing all terms of (5.3) in terms of the Chern classes $e_{k}$ of $E$ and using Lemma 5.3 [0546], we then use the fixed-point analysis for $E$ in \S 3.6 and the calculations in Proposition 3.27 [CBDB] and evaluate the Atiyah-Bott localization expression. We perform this computation in \S 7.
