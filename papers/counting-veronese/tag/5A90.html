Proof. (5A90)

We use upper semi-continuity, Proposition 3.15 [AD31], and Proposition 3.14 [2430]. The group $\PGL(3)$ acts on the scheme $\CT$. Under this action, it is easy to check that either the point $(T, T^{*})$ from Proposition 3.15 [AD31] or its symmetric flip (where we interchange $X,Y,Z$ with $\check{X},\check{Y},\check{Z}$ everywhere) is contained in the closure of every orbit. We conclude that the tangent space is $6$-dimensional at every point in $\CT$ by semi-continuity, and conclude by Proposition 3.14 [2430].
