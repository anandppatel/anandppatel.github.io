Corollary 3.17. (4A1A)

Let $S$ be a smooth projective surface and let $n \geq 2$. There exists a flat family of length-$\binom{n+1}{2}$ subschemes over the $\CT$-modification of $\Hilb_3 S$, resolving the $n$-th power map.
