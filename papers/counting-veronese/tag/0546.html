Lemma 5.3. (0546)

In the Chow ring $\CH^{\bullet}\CT$, we have $$\begin{aligned}[\lin(p)] &= c_{2}(1),\\ [\inc(p)] &= H^{2} - c_{2}(1) + 2c_{2}(2)-c_{2}(3).\end{aligned}$$
