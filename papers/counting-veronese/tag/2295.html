Proposition 3.25. (2295)

The weights of the $\Gm$ action on $E$ at the fixed points of type (1)-(6) are:

  1. $(5a, 5b, 5c, 4a+b, 4a+c, 4b+a, 4b+c, 4c+a, 4c+b)$,
  2. $(5a, 4a+b, 4a+c, 5c, 4c+a, 4c+b, 3c+a+b, 3c+2b, 2c+3b)$ and its 6 permutations,
  3. $(5b, 4b+a, 4b+c, 5c, 4c+a, 4c+b, 3c+a+b, 3c+2b, 2c+3b)$ and its 6 permutations,
  4. $(5c, 4c+b, 3c+2b, 2c+3b, c+4b, 5b, 4c+a, 3c+a+b, 2c+a+2b)$ and its 6 permutations,
  5. $(5c, 4c+a, 3c+2a, 4c+b, 3c+a+b, 2c+2a+b, 3c+2b, 2c+2b+a, 2c+3b)$ and its 6 permutations,
  6. $(5c, 4c+a, 3c+2a, 2c+3a, 4c+b, 3c+a+b, 3c+2b, 2c+2b+a, 2c+3b)$ and its 6 permutations.
