Definition 4.8. (0DB3)

We define the following subvarieties of $\BP(p)$:

  1. $\Dom(p)$ is the closure of the locus of $(T,T^{*},\Lambda) \in \BP(p)$ such that $p \notin T$ and $T \notin \Thin$.
  2. $\Inc(p)$ is the closure of the locus of $(T,T^{*},\Lambda)$ where $p \in T$ and $T$ is an honest triangle.
  3. $\Lin(p)$ is the closure of the locus of $(T,T^{*},\Lambda)$ where $T$ is contained in a line through $p$.
