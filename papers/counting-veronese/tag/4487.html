Remark 3.11. (4487)

The scheme structure on $\CT$ is induced by the condition $\Lambda_{T}^{\dagger} = \Lambda_{T^{*}}.$ Indeed, by Proposition 3.3 [C2E8] and Remark 3.2 [A215], the assignments $(T, T^{*}) \mapsto \Lambda_{T}^{\dagger}$ and $(T, T^{*}) \mapsto \Lambda_{T^{*}}$ yield two vector subbundles of the trivial bundle $\underline{H^{0}(\check{\P}^{2}, \O_{\check{\P}^{2}}(2))}$ over $\Hilb_{3}\P^{2} \times \Hilb_{3}\check{\P}^{2}$. Requiring these two subbundles to be equal yields the scheme structure on $\CT$. $\CT$'s functor of points is inherent in this description.
