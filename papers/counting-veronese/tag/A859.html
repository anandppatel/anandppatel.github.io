Theorem 2.3. (A859)

Let $(q_{1}, \dots, q_{13}) \in (\P^{9})^{13}$ be a general tuple with associated tuple $(p_{1}, \dots, p_{13}) \in (\P^{2})^{13}.$ There exists a bijective correspondence $$\begin{aligned}\left\{

$3$-uple Veronese
surfaces $V \subset \P^{9}$
containing $q_{1}, \dots , q_{13}$

\right\} \longleftrightarrow \left\{

Singular triads
$T \subset \P^{2}$
for $p_{1}, \dots, p_{13}$

\right\}.\end{aligned}$$
