Theorem 2.3. (A859)

Let $(q_{1}, \dots, q_{13}) \in (\P^{9})^{13}$ be a general tuple with associated tuple $(p_{1}, \dots, p_{13}) \in (\P^{2})^{13}.$ There exists a bijective correspondence $$\begin{aligned}\left\{ \begin{array}{c} $3$-uple Veronese\\ surfaces $V \subset \P^{9}$ \\ containing $q_{1}, \dots , q_{13}$ \end{array}\right\} \longleftrightarrow \left\{ \begin{array}{c} Singular triads \\ $T \subset \P^{2}$ \\ for $p_{1}, \dots, p_{13}$ \end{array} \right\}.\end{aligned}$$
