Definition 4.4. (1BD9)

We let $\Inf \subset \SQP$ denote the locus of triples $(T,T^{*}, \Lambda) \in \SQP$ where $\Base(\Lambda)$ has a $1$-dimensional component. We let $\Fin := \SQP \setminus \Inf$ denote the complement.
