Theorem 4.25. (2247)

Let $p_1, \dots, p_{13}$ be 13 general points in $\P^{2}$, and let $\Theta$ be as in Section 4.4. Then $$\Theta = \left\{ \begin{array}{c} $(T,T^{*}) \in\CT$ such that\\ $T$ is a singular triad \\ for $p_{1}, \dots, p_{13}$ \end{array} \right\}.$$
