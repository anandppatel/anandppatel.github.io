2.6. The operation $[M]_\hbar$

In this last subsection, we define our operation $[M]_\hbar$ for a $d \times n$ matrix $M$. The operator $$[M]_\hbar:A^\bullet_{GL_{r+1}}(\mb{P}^r)^{\otimes n} \to QH^\bullet_{GL_{r+1}}(\mb{P}^r)$$ is defined by taking the formal sum of convolutions (partial integrals to the last factor of $\mb{P}^r$) $$\begin{aligned}[M]_\hbar(f_1(H_1),\ldots,f_n(H_n))=\sum_{k=0}^{d-1}\left(\int_{(\mb{P}^r)^{n+1}\to \mb{P}^r}f_1(H_1)\ldots f_n(H_n)\opc{\tau^{\le k+1}(M\oplus \ast)}\right)\hbar^k\\\in A^\bullet_{GL_{r+1}}(\mb{P}^r)[\hbar]\cong QH^\bullet_{GL_{r+1}}(\mb{P}^r).\end{aligned}$$

See Section 4, where we exposit the geometric construction of the small equivariant quantum cohomology of $\mb{P}^r$, which parallels the above.
