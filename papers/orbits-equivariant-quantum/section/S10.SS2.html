10.2. Kronecker duals for general matrices and mod $F^k$

We will now investigate the Kronecker dual of $\opc{M}$ for a general $d \times n$ matrix $M$.

A general $d \times n$ matrix $M$ is realized as $\tau^{\le d}(\ast \oplus \ldots \oplus \ast)$, so by Lemma 10.3 [F065](a,b) and Theorem 10.4 [25D7](a) we have $$[M]_\hbar(f_1(H_1),\ldots,f_n(H_n))=\bar{f_1}(z)\ldots\bar{f_n}(z)\text{ mod }F(z)^d$$ whenever $f_1, \ldots, f_n$ are polynomials with coefficients in $A^\bullet_{GL_{r+1}}(\pt)$. Writing $\bar{f_1}(z)\ldots\bar{f_n}(z)=a_0+a_1F(z)+\ldots$, we have $$\opc{M}^\dagger(f_1(H_1),\ldots,f_n(H_n))=\int_{\mb{P}^r} a_{d-1}=[z^r][F(z)^{d-1}](\bar{f_1}(z)\ldots\bar{f_n}(z)\text{ mod }F(z)^d)$$ by Lemma 10.3 [F065](d).
