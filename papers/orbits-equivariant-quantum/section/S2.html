Section 2. Definitions

\label{NotandConv} In what follows, we fix an algebraically closed field $K$ of arbitrary characteristic. If $v$ is a nonzero element in a vector space $V$ we write $\wt{v}$ for the image of $v$ in $\mb{P}(V)$. We denote the projectivization of the vector space $\mb{A}^{k \times \ell}$ of $k \times \ell$ matrices by $\mb{P}^{k \times \ell-1}$.

To help the reader locate relevant symbols, we have included the tables Figure 2 and Figure 3 in Section 15.
