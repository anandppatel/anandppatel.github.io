Theorem 8.8. (2A68)

For a matroid $\MM$, we have the following expression for $1_{P_\MM}$ as a combination of indicator functions of Schubert matroid rank polytopes. $$\sum_{\ell=1}^n \sum_{\emptyset \subsetneq X_1 \subsetneq \ldots \subsetneq X_\ell=\{1,\ldots,n\}}(-1)^{n-\ell}1_{\operatorname{Sch}(\rk_{\MM}(X_1),\ldots,\rk_{\MM}(X_\ell),X_1,\ldots,X_\ell)}.$$
