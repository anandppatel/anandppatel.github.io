Definition 6.5. (276C)

We say that $M_D$ simply orb-limits to a matrix $M$ if there exist maps $\gamma_T: D\setminus\{0\}\to T$ and $\gamma_{GL}: D\setminus\{0\}\to GL_{d}$ such that $M=\lim_{p \to 0}\gamma_{GL}(p) M_D(p) \gamma_T(p) $. We say that $M_D$ orb-limits to a matrix $M$ if $M_D$ simply orb-limits to $M$ after pulling back to a finite cover by a nonsingular curve (possibly everywhere ramified) of an open neighborhood of $0\in D$.
