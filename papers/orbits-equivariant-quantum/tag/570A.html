Corollary 4.10. (570A)

The expression $$\sum_{|A|=d+1}\left(\prod_{j \not \in A}F(z_j)\right)\sum_{i \in A}\prod_{j \ne i}\frac{(-1)^{n-d+1}}{z_i-z_j}$$ is a polynomial in $z_1,\ldots,z_r$, and $\opc{M_{d+1}}$ is obtained by evaluating this polynomial at $z_i=H_i$.
