Proposition 7.8. (795C)

Let $M$ be a $d \times n$ rank $d$ matrix and let $C$ be a matroidal cone. Define the $n \times n$ matrix $B$ by $$B_{i,k}= \begin{cases} \lambda_{i,k} & \mbox{if $e_k-e_i \in C$ and $k \ne i$}\\ 0 & \mbox{otherwise} \end{cases}$$ where the $\lambda_{i,k}$ are general elements of $K$. Let $N = M(1+tB)$.

  1. For any $z \in \Vt(\Delta)$, the order $\ord_t \det(N^z)$ is the minimum value of $|z \setminus w|$ over all pairs $(w, \phi)$ such that $w \in \Vt(P_M)$ and $\phi: z \setminus w \to w \setminus z$ is a bijection with $e_k-e_{\phi(k)} \in C$ for all $k \in z \setminus w$. In particular, it is $\infty$ if and only if no such pair exists.
  2. For general $t$, the rank polytope of $N$ is $(P_A+C)\cap \Delta$.
