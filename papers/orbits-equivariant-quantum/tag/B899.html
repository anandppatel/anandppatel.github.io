Lemma 12.4. (B899)

With the setup of Lemma section, assume every line in $\mb{P}^r$ meets $X$ in at least three points. The general fiber of $$\begin{aligned}\phi_X : \mb{G}(1,r)\dashrightarrow \mb{P}({\rm Sym}^d (K^{\vee})^{2})//SL_{2}\end{aligned}$$ is closed in $\mb{G}(1,r)$ and has class $s^{*}\Phi_*\opc[1]{M}_{\mb{P}(\mc{S}^{\vee})^d}$, where $M$ is a general $2 \times d$ matrix and $\Phi$ is the multiplication map $$\begin{aligned}\Phi:\mb{P}(\mc{S}^{\vee})^d\to \mb{P}({\rm Sym}^d \mc{S}^{\vee}),\end{aligned}$$ where $\mb{P}(\mc{S}^{\vee})^d$ denotes the $d$-fold fiber product of $\mb{P}(\mc{S}^{\vee})$ with itself over $\mb{G}(1,r)$.
