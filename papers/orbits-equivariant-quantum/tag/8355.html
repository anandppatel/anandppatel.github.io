Lemma 9.5. (8355)

For any permutation $\sigma \in S_n$ and any $d \times n$ matrix $M$, define $B(\sigma)$ as in the statement of Theorem 9.2 [C75C]. Then we have $P_M + C_\sigma = e_{B(\sigma)} + C_\sigma$.
