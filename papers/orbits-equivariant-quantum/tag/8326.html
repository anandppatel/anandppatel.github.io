Lemma 8.6. (8326)

Let $M$ be a general $1\times 2$ matrix, corresponding to two identical points in $\mb{P}^0$. Then $\op[r][2]{M}$ is the diagonal cycle in $\mb{P}^r \times \mb{P}^r$, and its class is given by $\frac{F(H_1)-F(H_2)}{H_1-H_2}$.
