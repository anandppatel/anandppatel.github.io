Definition 7.2. (F82F)

Let $M$ be a $d \times n$ matrix and let $C$ be a matroidal cone. We define the associated polytope of $(M,C)$ to be the polytope $P_M+C \subset \{\sum x_i=d\}$. We say that $(M, C)$ is an admissible pair if $P_{M} + C$ is full-dimensional in $\{\sum x_i = d\}$ and $(P_M + C) \cap \Delta = P_M$.
