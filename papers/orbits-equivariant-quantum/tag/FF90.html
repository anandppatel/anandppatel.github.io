Lemma 8.7. (FF90)

If $M \in K^{d \times (n+1)}$ is a series-parallel matrix with distinguished column $c$, then letting $z$ denote the hyperplane variable associated to $c$ and $H_1,\ldots,H_n$ the remaining hyperplane variables, there are rational functions $Q_{M,i}(x_1,\ldots, x_n,y_1,\ldots, y_n)$ not depending on $r$ or $F$ such that the polynomial expression for $\opc{M}$ of degree at most $r$ in each $H_i$ and $z$ can be written as (formally simplifying to a polynomial before evaluating at $H_i$ and $z$) $$\sum Q_{M,i}(H_1,\ldots,H_n,F(H_1),\ldots,F(H_n))\frac{F(z)-F(H_i)}{z-H_i}.$$
