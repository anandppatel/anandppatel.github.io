Proof. (2829)

Lemma simage implies the fiber of $\wt{\mc{O}}\to D$ over $p\in D$ is $\overline{\mb{A}^{r\times d}M_D(p)T}$ for $p\in D$ general provided the map is generically finite, which is equivalent to the dimension statement. To prove the statement of the special fiber over $0\in D$, it suffices to prove it after a finite cover of a neighborhood of $0\in D$. After this replacement, we get maps $\gamma_T:D\to T$ and $\gamma_{GL}: D\to GL_d$ such that the matrix $M=(\gamma_{GL_d} M_D \gamma_{T})(0)$. Then $\overline{\mb{A}^{(r+1)\times d}MT}\times \{0\}$ is contained in the closure of the image of $\phi$.
