Proof. (C8CD)

Let $V$ be a 2-dimensional vector space over $K$. The multiplication map gives a $GL_{2}$-equivariant map $$\begin{aligned}\Phi: \mb{P}(V^{\vee})^d\to \mb{P}({\rm Sym}^d V^{\vee}),\end{aligned}$$ which induces a map of equivariant chow rings $$\begin{aligned}A_{GL_2}^{\bullet}(\mb{P}(V^{\vee})^d)\leftarrow A_{GL_2}^{\bullet}\mb{P}({\rm Sym}^d V^{\vee})\end{aligned}$$
