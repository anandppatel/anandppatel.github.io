Definition 2.9. (5A7E)

We say that a $d \times n$ matrix $M$ is connected if $\rk(M) = d$ and there does not exist $\varnothing \subsetneq A \subsetneq \{1, \ldots, n\}$ such that $\rk_M(A) + \rk_M(\{1, \ldots, n\} \setminus A) = d$.
