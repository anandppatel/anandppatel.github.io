Theorem 5.1. (1CA9)

{Binglin}}] Let $\phi_i:W \to W_i$ be nonzero linear maps, and let $U_i = \phi^*(W_i^\vee)\subseteq W_i^\vee $. Consider the rational map $$\mu:\mb{P}(W) \dashrightarrow \prod \mb{P}(W_i).$$ Then we have $$\mu_*(H^j)=\sum \prod_{i=1}^n H_i^{\dim(W_i)-1-e_i}$$ where the sum is over all tuples $(e_1,\ldots,e_n) \in \mb{Z}^n$ such that $$\begin{aligned}&\sum_{i \in A}e_i < \dim \sum_{i \in A} U_i\qquad\text{for all nonempty $A\subseteq [n]$ and}\\ &\sum_{i=1}^n e_i=\dim(W)-1-j.\end{aligned}$$
