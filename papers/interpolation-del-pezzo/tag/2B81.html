Definition 8.1. (2B81)

Let $X \subset \bp^n$ be projective scheme with a fixed embedding into projective space which lies on a unique irreducible component of the Hilbert scheme. Define $\hilb X$ to be the irreducible component of the Hilbert scheme on which $[X]$ lies, taken with reduced scheme structure.
